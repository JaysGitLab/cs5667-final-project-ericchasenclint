The Process

Select one team from each seed. Enter a score for the championship game for tiebraker purposes. Submit your selections!

If your team wins, you receive points! For each subsequent round your team wins, you receive even more points.

Scoring:

Round 1 - 10 points
Round 2 - 20 points
Sweet Sixteen - 30 points
Elite Eight - 40 points
Final Four - 50 points
Championship - 60 points

Bonus Points:

You win bonus points if one of your teams beats a lower numbered seed before the Final Four. For example, if your seed 10 team beats a seed 7 team, you will earn 3 bonus points.
